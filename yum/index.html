Serves 12

SINSTER KID LEMON

POPPY SEED CAKE

Prep Time: 15 Minutes

Cook Time: 35 Minutes

Ready In: 50 Minutes

Ingrediants

  • 18.25 oz. package lemon cake mix
  • 3.4 oz.package of instant vanilla pudding
  • 4 Eggs
  • 3/4 Cup of Vegetable Oil
  • 1 1/4 Cup of Water
  • 1/5 Cup of Poppy Seeds
  • 21 oz. can of lemon pie filling
  • 3.4 oz. package of instant lemon pudding mix
  • 1 Cup of Milk
  • 8 oz. container of frozen whipped topping,thawed

Steps

  1. 1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease and use 1 Tablespoon to flour a 9 by 13 in pan.
  2. 2. In a large bowl, stir together cake mix and vanilla pudding mix. Make a well in the center and pour in eggs, oil, water, and poppy seeds. Beat on low speed until blended. Scrape bowl, and beat 4 minutes on medium speed. Pour batter into prepared pan.
  3. 3. Bake in the preheated oven for 35 to 40 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean. Allow to cool completely. Spread lemon pie filling over the cooled cake.
  4. 4. In a large bowl, mix instant lemon pudding with milk. Mix well and let stand about 5 Minutes until fairly set. Fold in thawed whipped topping. Spread over pie filling. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours before serving.
